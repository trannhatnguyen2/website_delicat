Return Policy

If you need to return an item, you can do so free of charge and within 30 days of purchase. All returned items need to be unworn, unsoiled and in a saleable condition with all labels and tags still attached.
• Returns to Vietnamese are free
• 30 days return period
• Returns process takes less than a minute
• Sustainable choice paperless returns & store drop-offs
• Multiple convenient return options
• Monitor the status of your return with tracking and notifications

Please note: our couriers are only able to accept returns up to these maximum dimensions. Any packages larger will need to be sent back as multiple items.
• DPD accepts parcels up to 20kg, max 60 x 60 x 60 cm.
• InPost accepts parcels up to 15kg, max 41cm x 38cm x 64cm.
• CollectPlus accepts parcels up to 10kg, 60cm x 50cm x 50cm.
• Choose MyDHL for all parcels exceeding 20kgs and bigger than 60 x 60 x 60 cm. This is a home collection service.

• Process orders that you have submitted and inform you of the status of the order;
• Carry out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into by you and us;
• Deal with entries into a competition;
• Seek your views or comments on the services we provide;
• Notify you of changes to our services;
• Send you communications which you have requested and that may be of interest to you. These may include information about sales campaigns, activities, promotions of our associated companies’ goods and services;
• Develop, test and improve our systems, services and the products we provide;

We review our retention periods for personal information on a regular basis. We are legally required to hold some types of information to fulfill our statutory obligations. We will hold your personal information on our systems for as long as is necessary for the relevant activity, or as long as is set out in any relevant contract you hold with us. We will not sell or rent your information to third parties.



Delivery Policy

Orders are dispatched 6 days a week (Sun - Mon), excluding Bank Holidays. Orders placed after 5pm Friday or on Saturday will be dispatched on Sunday.
We will send you an email confirmation once your order has been dispatched. Unfortunately it is not possible to change the delivery address of the order once it has left our warehouse, however if you do need to amend your order prior to dispatch then please get in touch with our Customer Service team who will do their best to accommodate any changes.
We strive to ensure our delivery information on site is as accurate as possible. However, on some occasions, packages may be delayed due to factors out of our control.

Standard Delivery
Delivery Tracked Standard Delivery is free on all UK Mainland orders over £70 and your order will be delivered within 7 working days. For orders under £70, the Standard Delivery option is £4.95 dispatched via Evri.

Evri will send additional delivery updates by email. If you provide a phone number, they will also send an SMS to pre-advise you of the delivery time.

If you don’t receive your parcel within the projected timeframe after we send you a dispatch email, please contact us. You have 30 days after receipt of a dispatch email to contact us in event of a non-delivery. Refunds cannot be made after this time.
