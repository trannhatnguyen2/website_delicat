OUR STORY

ABOUT US

Délicat in French means delicate, elegant, elegant. Délicat is the brand name and the goal that we set when building the brand. With meticulously tailored clothing, it always shows elegance and sophistication but still retains natural beauty. Therefore, each product at Délicat, each suit, shirt or pants is a harmony between luxurious materials, tailoring, sophisticated style and knowledge of fashion.

MISSION & VISION

MISSION

For us, the most important thing is quality. To create beautiful suits, we use only the best fabrics and materials on the market. Fabric samples are provided by famous manufacturers in the world from UK and Italy. Not only that, each of our services is made by carpenters with at least 30 years of experience in the profession. Through our passionate passion for beautiful clothing, we help our clients build elegant and compact wardrobes, and direct them to their naturally cultivated style. Besides clothing, we also source and supply a growing range of other unique and handmade items that contribute to 'a good life'.?

VISION

With more and more complete life and increasing demand for clothing, the market is more and more open. That means we will have more opportunities, with the motto, goal and mission set out, Délicat will become the leading company in Vietnam trusted and chosen by customers.
